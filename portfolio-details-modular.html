School visits (modular based)

Workshop information

  • Each workshop comprises of:
    • - Basic Animal Care or Welfare Talk
    • - One hands-on activity
    • - Supervised interaction with animals

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Price: Contact us for more information
  • No of participants required: 10

Aim

To inculcate a love for animals in our youths (11-16Y), equipping them with knowledge on animal welfare.

During the event, students will learn:

  1. Basic pet care
  2. Basic animal handling skills
  3. Knowledge of constructing animal enrichment toys

We aim to inculcate empathy among students and equip them with the knowledge on animal welfare so that they can spearhead their own animal welfare projects such as fundraising for shelters/rescues and educating the public what they have learnt on animal welfare.
